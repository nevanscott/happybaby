Welcome to the Happy Baby movie website. We're shooting a movie in New York City, based on the novel Happy Baby, beginning May 14th and wrapping June 8th. And we'd love to get you involved. The funding for Happy Baby came almost exclusively through crowdsourcing. We raised $85,000 (on Kickstarter)[http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/706884381/happy-baby-the-movie] to make this movie (well, actually $93,000, but then Kickstarter and Amazon fees took us back to $85,000). After the Kickstarter some more money came in, including a $50,000 investment, for a total production budget of $152,000. It's a lot of money, but at the same time, it's not very much. It's not enough money to pay the director, for example. Happy Baby is a sprawling story with many characters and locations. The movie is divided into five parts, each one earlier than the other, so we're dealing with five different dates: 2014, 2009, 2005, 1995, and 1991. That means we have three different actors just to play the protagonist. We have a scene in a juvenile detention center with forty boys. We need six different apartments, an office, a detention center, a convenience store, and a bus. From the beginning, starting with fundraising, and continuing with casting, this has been a collaborative process. It's going to be really hard, and a lot of fun.
